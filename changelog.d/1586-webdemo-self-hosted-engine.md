- **Web demo: self-host the Filament/SceneView engine and stop bricking on a model-CDN miss ([#1586](https://github.com/sceneview/sceneview/issues/1586)).** `samples/web-demo`'s `index.html` loaded `filament.js` and `sceneview.js` from `cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/sceneview/sceneview@main/...` — a jsDelivr hiccup 404'd both engine scripts and turned the whole Playwright suite red. Both files (plus `filament.wasm`) are now bundled under `src/jsMain/resources/js/` and referenced by relative path, so they ship with `jsBrowserDistribution` and the deployed `/web-demo/`. The default catalog model load is also decoupled from engine init: a flaky model-CDN miss now surfaces as a transient chip instead of a fatal "Failed to initialize" overlay, keeping the viewer usable.
