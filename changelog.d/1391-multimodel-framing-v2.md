- iOS: the `Multi-Model Park` demo now frames all four streamed models centered and correctly sized. The previous union-AABB fix latched the camera framing as soon as two consecutive ticks saw an unchanged bounds diagonal — which trivially happened inside the multi-second gap between two streamed models landing — so the camera froze on a partial 1-2-model union and the rest of the scene streamed in off-screen, leaving a black viewport. Framing now latches only once the content-union AABB has held steady for a sustained wall-clock window that spans the inter-model streaming gaps, and the framing driver runs long enough to cover the full 30-70 s streamed-load window. Single-model demos (Model Viewer, Geometry) are unaffected (#1391, #1514, #1385).
